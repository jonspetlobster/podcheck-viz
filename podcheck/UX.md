### 6.4 Design Principles

1. **Reference tool, not tabloid.** No clickbait. No gotcha energy. Just facts with sources.
2. **Data density done right.** Show a lot of information but with clear hierarchy.
3. **Wikipedia meets IMDb.** Authoritative. Structured. Browseable. Linkable.
4. **Neutral tone.** Colors are informational (green = accurate, red = inaccurate), not emotional.
5. **Source everything.** Every number links to evidence. Trust is earned through transparency.
